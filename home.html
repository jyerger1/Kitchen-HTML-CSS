ANDREW'S FAVORITE RESTAURANTS

CHARLOTTE


  1. NODA BODEGA

    2. WEBSITE | MENU | REVIEWS

    NODA Bodega has some of the best sandwiches in Charlotte. It's a great place to grab a sandwich and then hit the local breweries!


  2. SALUD BEER SHOP

    3. WEBSITE | MENU | REVIEWS

    Salud Beer Shop has one of the best beer selections in Charlotte. It's the perfect place to spend a night out drinking beer and eating some amazing pizza.


  3. KILLER B'S

    4. WEBSITE | MENU | REVIEWS

    If you're looking for some of the best food trucks in Charlotte, Killer B's is at the top of the list. You wont find a better smoked brisket or tofu taco anywhere else.


  4. DISH

    5. WEBSITE | MENU | REVIEWS

    As someone originally from Mississippi, I've eaten a lot of home style southern food. If you're looking for authentic southern fried chicken or a great brunch, check out Dish.


  5. THE CELLAR AT DUCKWORTH'S

    6. WEBSITE | MENU | REVIEWS

    The Cellar at Duckworth's is an incredible high-end restaurant in Charlotte. If you're looking for a night out drinking fancy cocktails and a nice steak, check out The Cellar at Duckworth's.
